Madam Boutique

The Store of woman fashion accessories since 1982

With over 40 years of experience, Madam Boutique continues its legacy of providing superior fabric, value for money and trendsetting apparel to its consumers. It all began in 1982 when the textile industry had not even flourished to its complete potential, that Madam Boutique initiated ready-made winter wear, particularly knits for men, women and children. From then on, Madam Boutique became a prestigious brand and won respect as the master of detail in the realm of ready-made garments manufacturing. With over 80+ outlets across Pakistan, Madam Boutique stands as an enormous textile giant today. However, looking at the market potential amongst clothing brands for women, Madam Boutique further more ventured into creating its exclusive women’s clothing line in 2012 which has now become a one-stop clothing solution for the women of Pakistan. Thus, we became to be known as Madam Boutique. The umbrella brand is now clearly divided into two: Madam Boutique – Women’s Formal Clothing, Knits, and Ethnic Wear; Women’s Prêt, Unstitched, and Accessories.
